Types of Brazilian Investments

Direct Treasure

Tesouro Direto is a National Treasury Program developed in partnership with B3 for the sale of federal government bonds to individuals, 100% online. Launched in 2002, the Program was created with the objective of democratizing access to public bonds, allowing investments from R$ 30.00 onwards.

CDB

The CDB is one of the best known investments for fixed income Brazilian investidor. This is mainly due to the profitability and security of the Bank Deposit Certificate. It is nothing more than a bond issued by banks with the aim of raising money.

LCI e LCA

LCI (Letter of Credit in Real Estate) and LCA (Letter of Credit for Agribusiness) are two types of income tax-exempt fixed income investments, which usually guarantee returns well above those of savings accounts of Brazilian.

Company Shares

Shares represent a fraction of a company's share capital. When buying a share, the investor becomes a partner in the company, that is, in a business. You start to run the risks of this business as well as participate in the profits and losses like any entrepreneur.

Investment Funds

The administration and management of the Fund are carried out by trained professionals (managers), and the Investment Fund is governed by regulations. This investment alternative has several advantages compared to investing individually.
